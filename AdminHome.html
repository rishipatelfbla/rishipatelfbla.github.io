Welcome Admin,

As an administrator at Perry High School, your job is to assist students in making the right academic choices as well as helping them balance their personal and academic lives. With the help of PHS Job Finder, you can guide your students in the right direction by selecting suitable job postings that you know will meet their needs.

By clicking on the Applications page button, you will be directed to a page that has a table with available job postings. You can also add more job postings that businesses have sent you using the Submit Job Postings page; additionally, you can edit and delete any job postings.
