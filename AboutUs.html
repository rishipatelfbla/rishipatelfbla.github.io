About Us!

RIO is a tourism company based in Dubai, UAE. RIO was founded on
September 1st, 2022. Our founders lose their jobs during the COVID-19
pandemic, and then start this company. Currently, RIO only provides
tourism services in Dubai, and Abu Dhabi. But in the coming years,
we are planning to expand our services to other country and famous
places around the globe. Thanks to our great customers and employees,
we as a company have grew alot. And we hope you would like to
become a part of my RIO family, by booking your next Dubai trip with us.

Enjoy your trip!
